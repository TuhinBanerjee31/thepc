Brief about PMI Certification.

The Project Management Institute (PMI) awards Project Management Professional Certification, also known as PMI certification, to professionals who meet certain education and experience requirements. Applicants must have at least 7,500 hours of project management experience plus 35 hours of related education, or a relevant four-year degree plus 4,500 hours of project management experience and 35 hours of project management training. The Project Management Institute has thousands of members from all over the world, and their Project Management Professional Certification is a globally recognized designation.
